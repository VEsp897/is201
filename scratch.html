Why this era feels important

ARIRANG feels emotionally bigger than a regular comeback. The album title connects BTS to a traditional Korean song associated with longing, resilience, and cultural identity, which makes this era feel both personal and symbolic.

Hardships that shaped the return

The years surrounding the group comeback included solo work, enlistment, time away from full-group promotions, and the pressure of returning with a project that felt worthy of that wait. That history gives the new era extra weight.

About Me

I am a Pre-Business student at Brigham Young University preparing for a future in corporate finance. I am especially drawn to work that combines financial discipline, strategic thinking, and measurable business impact.

My recent experiences have strengthened my interest in finance through real-world analysis, reporting, and business problem solving. During my internship with Heartland Landscape Group, I contributed to financial analysis related to sustainable product strategy and process improvement. I also gained reporting and operations experience through an on-campus internship with Lifey and worked on strategic modeling in a Walmart case competition.

Outside the classroom, I value leadership, service, and learning. I am fluent in Spanish, currently learning Korean, and enjoy music, cultural arts, languages, and calligraphy.

Career Direction

My goal is to build a career in corporate finance where I can support decision making through thoughtful analysis, clear reporting, and long-term value creation.

What I Bring

A strong work ethic, attention to detail, quantitative curiosity, and a growing foundation in accounting, finance, and business communication.
